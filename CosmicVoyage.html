Cosmic Voyage 2025

About the Event

Cosmic Voyage is our annual astronomy fest, celebrating the wonders of the universe. The event features captivating talks by eminent astronomers, hands-on workshops, and stargazing sessions.

Date: November 2025

Venue: IIT Roorkee Campus

Past Editions

  • 2024 - Featured talks on exoplanets and dark matter
  • 2023 - Night-long observation of meteor showers
  • 2022 - Special workshop on astrophotography
